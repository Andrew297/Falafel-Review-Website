Edinburgh Falafel Review

Yum Yum Kurdish Kebab House

My current favourite place to get falafel in Edinburgh. Great wrap and salad - a particular highlight being the spicy onions. Various different combinations are available - baba ghanoush and hummus as well as lots of roasted vegetables are options on the menu.

Price: £5.50

Poppy

Coffee shop on Hamilton Place in Stockbridge that has falafel on the menu! Only one person working there but she was really friendly and the wrap was ready pretty quickly. Comes with olives, sweet chilli sauce and hummus. The only downside is that the wrap wasn't any bigger!

Price: £4.50

Nile Valley Cafe

African Cafe with several falafel wraps and very reasonable prices. I had the falafel, hummus, and roasted aubergine wrap. The salad was nothing special but the other ingredients were tasty, especially the aubergine. Suffered a seagull attack while walking with the wrap so it must have looked good!

Price: £4.50

Hajars Shwarma House

Kebab shop near Tollcross with quite a few falafel options. Very pickle heavy salad which I definitely enjoyed. The salad also contains mint leaves which add an interesting flavour. Slightly bizarre falafel meal option which consists of a huge amount of hummus and 8 falafel balls for £7.50 available if thats your thing too.

Price: £4.99 (small) or £5.99 (large)

Pizza Paradise

Really nice green shop next to Greyfriar's Bobby. Really good wraps - particularly enjoyed the chilli sauce and the hummus here. Unfortunately haven't seen it open recently - might be a good plan to head up the road to Yum Yum Kurdish Kebab House
